Bhandup Athletes Club

Bhandup Athletes Club, led by Coach Navin Sir, a Navy officer, is a premier training facility known for producing top-tier athletes for army and government positions. With a track record spanning five years, Coach Navin Sir's expertise guides individuals from beginners to state and national levels, honing their skills for success.

Bhandup Athletes Club

Coach: Navin Sir

Fees: 500/month

Timings: 6am to 9am

Address: Bhandup Powai RunningXCycling track
